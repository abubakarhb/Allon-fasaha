Mission

  • The Challenge in Nigeria

    7.2 million Out of School Children miss out on access to decent employment. Nigeria loses out on the literate and skilled workforce it needs to grow economically

  • Why it exists ?

    Learning is limited to classrooms excluding millions of children

    Education is free but not accessible to 7.2million “Out of School” children in Northern Nigeria

    A dearth of qualified teachers (Average Teacher to Student ratio is 1:51)

  • The Solution!

    Allon Fasaha is for Out-of-School children who do not have access to formal education and lose out on future opportunities. It's a mobile learning platform that provides accelerated learning and integrates the “out-of-school” back into the formal education system.
