Allon Fasaha is a program powered by Steamledge Community that targets and aims at giving out-of-school children access to basic education so they dont miss out on future opportunities of living a standard life.

The program aims at impacting the lives of the estimated 17million out-of-school children in Nigeria, through the Learn & Feed for Basic Education, Highschool Scholarships, and Skill Acquisition Programs.
